# Landing Legal PDF Outline

Use this outline for downloadable legal assets unless the user requests a different structure.

## Standard outline

1. Title and short promise.
2. Who this guide is for.
3. What problem it helps identify.
4. Key legal orientation, written in plain Spanish.
5. Practical steps or checklist.
6. Common mistakes or red flags.
7. When to consult a lawyer.
8. CTA: contact Tamara & Piñeros for case-specific review.
9. Disclaimer: informational material, not personalized legal advice.

## Tone

Professional, clear, calm, and practical. Avoid fear-based marketing. Avoid overpromising results.

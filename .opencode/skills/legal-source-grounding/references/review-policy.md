# Legal Source Grounding Review Policy

All legal PDFs, guides, checklists, intake documents, disclaimers, and compliance copy for Tamara & Piñeros require human legal review by default.

## Source hierarchy

1. Explicit user-approved legal source or firm policy.
2. Current Colombian legal text provided by the user or verified during the task.
3. Existing project legal artifacts already reviewed by the user.
4. General legal orientation clearly marked as non-personalized.

If no source exists, the claim must be marked `Needs source` and must not be stated as legal fact.

## Required review block

Every artifact must include internal drafting notes with:

- Human legal review required: yes/no.
- If no, quote the user's waiver.
- Claims needing source or lawyer validation.
- Disclaimer status.

## Mandatory disclaimer posture

Client-facing PDFs should state that the material is informative and does not replace personalized legal advice. Use neutral professional Spanish unless the user requests another language.
